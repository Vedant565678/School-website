WHY CHOOSE EMPROS INTERNATIONL SCHOOL ?

Best Education
Top Managment
Holistic Learning

  • Best Education

    ASM's Empros International School is one of the best CBSE schools in Pune. With 2 sprawling campuses, one in Talegaon and another in Chinchwad, the schools boast of lush green settings in juxtaposition with state-of-the-art learning facilities to help nurture the minds of our students.

  • Top Managment

    Our team of highly trained and experienced educators, works in collaboration with the students, to provide truly experiential learning. In keeping with our commitment to provide holistic education to students, our curriculum has been designed to keep in pace with modern sensibilities while inspiring an intrinsic motivation to learn. We believe each child is unique and we want to help them discover their own true potential and talents.

  • Holistic Learning

    The EIS curriculum focuses not only on core academics but also on the intellectual, physical, social, emotional, and spiritual development of students. We believe that in order to be equipped for the real world, students need to look beyond good grades and develop their soft skills like creative thinking, problem-solving and critical thinking.
